Introducing California's Greatest Cliffs

What is so unique about California cliffs?

California cliffs are unique due to their dramatic vertical rise, often composed of soft soils prone to unpredictable landslides towards the sea, making them particularly susceptible to erosion, especially when combined with the state's active tectonic activity and the threat of rising sea levels. Explore our website to learn more about what we offer.

What are these cliffs?

Located within a 100 mile radius from Irvine, California, they can be reached by roads easily. The closest cliff to Irvine is West Cliff, which is the most popular and most visited.
